🔄 Restore Purchases

If you previously purchased Remember Pro and it's not showing as active:

  • Open Remember
  • Go to Settings (bottom-right tab)
  • Tap Remember Pro
  • Tap Restore Purchases

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used when you originally purchased.

💳 Subscription & Billing

All purchases are handled by Apple. To manage, cancel, or get a refund for your subscription:

  • Manage subscription: iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Remember
  • Request a refund: reportaproblem.apple.com

🤖 AI Features Not Showing?

Remember Pro's AI features require Apple Intelligence to be enabled on your device. Go to iOS Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri → and make sure it's turned on. AI features are only available on compatible devices.

📱 Widgets Not Updating?

  • Long-press the widget → Edit Widget to reconfigure
  • Remove and re-add the widget to your home screen
  • Open the main Remember app to force a data refresh

🗑️ Accidentally Deleted Something?

Remember has a 4-second undo window — a toast appears at the bottom of the screen after any deletion. Tap Undo quickly to restore it.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does Remember sync across devices? Yes — if iCloud is enabled in iOS Settings, your data syncs automatically via CloudKit.
  • Is my data private? Yes — all data stays on your device (and optionally your own iCloud). We collect nothing. See our Privacy Policy.
  • What is Remember Pro? A paid upgrade offering unlimited content, all themes, all widgets, Weekly Review, and AI features. Available as Monthly ($1.49), Yearly ($9.99 with 7-day free trial), or Lifetime ($29.99).
  • Can I use Remember on iPad? Yes — the app is optimized for iPad with a sidebar-adaptable layout.
  • How does the daily reminder rotation work? Remember uses a smart rotation algorithm that ensures all your reminders get shown over time, with configurable frequency weights.
